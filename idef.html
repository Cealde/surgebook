Defenses

After the Grace Period ends many teams just have a simple deepslate protection for their jigsaw, this maybe sometimes effective, but a good defense is key for winning

Making a defense around the jigsaw is mandatory, but its not the only way of defense. Deepslate now drops 2-3 cobbled deepslate making the amount of strong blocks very abundant and can be used to build some structures that may aid you such as:

A tower is good for a person with a bow and many arrows raining them on enemies trying to get middle-control or bridging towards their base, this is just one example of a good defensive setup, many of these types of defenses will make a base practically impenetrable.

However, attacking is not completely useless in these scenarios, the enemy can use an invisibility fragment or dig a tunnel through the caves.
